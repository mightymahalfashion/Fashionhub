Frequently Asked Questions

Back to Home

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our products and services.

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD). Credit/Debit card payments will be available soon.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for 1-2 business days delivery.

Can I return or exchange items?

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be in original condition with tags attached.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also check your order status in your account dashboard.

Shipping Policy

Shipping Information

We are committed to delivering your orders quickly and safely. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping process:

1

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email once your order is confirmed.

2

Packaging

Your items are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive in perfect condition.

3

Shipping

Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking number to monitor your package's progress.

4

Delivery

Your order will be delivered to your specified address during business hours.

Shipping Options

  • Standard Shipping (3-5 business days): Free for orders over ₹500
  • Express Shipping (1-2 business days): ₹99 additional charge
  • Same Day Delivery: Available in select cities for ₹199

Shipping Areas

We currently ship to all major cities across India. Remote areas may require additional 1-2 days for delivery.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be in original condition with tags attached
  • Original packaging must be included
  • Items should be unworn and unwashed
  • Return request must be initiated within 07 days
  • Certain items like undergarments and cosmetics are non-returnable

How to Return

1

Initiate Return

Log into your account and select the item you want to return from your order history.

2

Schedule Pickup

Choose a convenient time for our delivery partner to pick up the item from your address.

3

Quality Check

Once we receive the item, our team will inspect it to ensure it meets return conditions.

4

Refund Processing

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after quality check approval.
